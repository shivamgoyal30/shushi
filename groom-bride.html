Our Story

Hey, want to hear the story of how we met and ended up getting married? It all started with a random encounter that turned into something pretty special.

  • First We Meet

    July 3, 2017

    On my first day at work, surrounded by a room full of new faces, she took the seat in front of mine and simply said, "Hi!" Little did I know, that casual greeting would kickstart a journey beyond the confines of that training room, shaping the story of us.

  • The Start of It

    October 4, 2017

    Amidst the festive hum of office birthday celebrations, I found myself in my usual corner, observing the merriment from a distance. To my surprise, she approached with a bright smile and a simple request: "Wish me!" As our acquaintance took a sweet turn with a slice of birthday cake, little did I realize that this seemingly ordinary moment would mark the sweet beginning of our extraordinary journey together. ❤️

  • First Date

    December 25, 2017

    Alright, picture this: early morning, random plan to hit up Bangla Sahib with work buddies. We started as a group of five, but by 5:30 am, it was just us two left standing. No biggie, we rolled with the punches, braved the cold, and ended up in a nearby church. And just like that, our not-so-planned first date became a cool memory.

  • Devil's Circuit

    March 17, 2018

    So, there we were, tackling Devil's Circuit – a military obstacle course I'd never think of doing, if not for her encouragement. Navigating obstacles together, we faced challenges head-on, helping each other out along the way. Little did I know, it was a sneak peek into the teamwork that would define our journey.

  • Chopta Trek

    March 29, 2018

    Enter the Chopta trek – something everyone who knows me swore I'd never attempt. Yet, when she asked, my automatic response was a hesitant yes. As we started the trek, she exuded eagerness, while my primary goal was just to survive this unexpected adventure successfully. The Expedia friends group was there, all working together, but my attention was singularly focused on her. Throughout the journey to the top, we found ourselves naturally helping each other. Little did I know, and only much later did we realize, those shared moments became the tipping point for her to understand my not-so-obvious crush.

Shivam & Shruti
